# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product insights across different store locations and time periods.

This project demonstrates how Excel can be used as a powerful Business Intelligence tool for **data cleaning, analysis, and dashboarding**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand:

- Total sales performance  
- Customer footfall trends  
- Peak ordering hours  
- Best-selling products  
- Category contribution to revenue  
- Store location comparison  
- Weekday vs weekend order behavior  

The dashboard is fully interactive using **slicers and charts**, allowing dynamic filtering by **Month** and **Day**.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Questions Answered

- What is the **total revenue and footfall**?
- At what **time of day** are orders highest?
- Which **product categories** generate the most sales?
- What **drink sizes** are most popular?
- Which **store location** performs best?
- Which **products** are top revenue generators?
- How do **weekday orders** vary?

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### ⏰ Quantity Ordered Based on Hours
- Identifies peak hours for coffee orders
- Helps in staff scheduling and inventory planning

### 🥐 Category Sales Distribution
Shows % contribution of:
- Coffee  
- Bakery  
- Tea  
- Chocolate products  
- Coffee beans & flavours  

### 🥤 Size Distribution
Customer preference for:
- Large  
- Regular  
- Small  
- Not Defined  

### 🏪 Store Location Performance
Comparison of:
- Astoria  
- Hell’s Kitchen  
- Lower Manhattan  
Based on **Sales vs Footfall**

### ⭐ Top 5 Products by Sales
Top revenue drivers:
- Barista Espresso  
- Brewed Chai Tea  
- Hot Chocolate  
- Gourmet Brewed Coffee  
- Brewed Black Tea  

### 📅 Orders on Weekdays
Analyzes ordering pattern from Sunday → Saturday
